Dumplings

Our dumplings are a true celebration of flavor tradition and craftsmanship. As the heart of our menu and our signature meal these bite-sized delights are crafted with the finest ingredients and hand-folded to perfection. Whether you choose the classic pork and chive tender chicken or a medley of fresh seasonal vegetables every bite offers a rich savory experience that is hard to resist. Available in steamed pan-fried or boiled options each dumpling has its own unique texture and flavor from the softness of steamed to the crispy finish of pan-fried. We pair them with our house-made dipping sauces ranging from tangy soy-vinegar blends to spicy chili oil enhancing the layers of flavor in every bite. Our dumplings are more than just a dish they are our customer favorite and our pride. Whether you are enjoying them as a meal with friends or a quick snack you will understand why they have become the go-to choice for anyone who visits us. This signature offering embodies the comfort and tradition we strive to bring with every dish.

Porridge

Our porridge is the epitome of comfort food—a warm nourishing dish that is as soothing as it is satisfying. Made from high-quality grains slowly simmered to a creamy texture each bowl is carefully crafted to bring you a hearty and wholesome meal. Whether you are craving a simple traditional porridge or one enhanced with a variety of savory toppings like tender meats fresh vegetables or fragrant herbs we have something to suit every taste. As our most popular dish it has won the hearts of many customers with its rich comforting flavor and endless customization options. Each bowl is served in generous portions making it a perfect choice for any time of the day whether you're starting your morning or enjoying a comforting dinner. Our porridge embodies the warmth and tradition of our kitchen offering you a meal that is both satisfying and deeply rooted in culinary heritage. Experience the dish that has become a favorite among our guests and find out why it's loved by so many.
